Dream world

  • Omori. The main character of the game and younger sister of Mari. He does not speak in the game and rarely shows emotion. He is an alter ego of Sunny that was created to protect him from the bad memories and trauma he has. He is negative and appears to hate Sunny. He is around 12.

  • Hero. Older brother of Kel, he is shown to be caring and responsible. He enjoys cooking and looks after the other kids in the group. He also appears to like Mari. He is 15.

  • Aubrey. She is hotheaded and very strongwilled. She enjoys dressing up often has arguments with Kel that Hero has to break up. She is 12.

  • Kel. Hero's younger brother, he is athletic and an airhead. He is silly and easygoing and greatly looks up to his older brother. He is 12.

  • Basil. He is gentle and quiet, and loves gardening and taking care of plants. He appears to have trouble making friends sometimes but is very kind and caring. He also has a photo album that Mari gave him and likes taking pictures of his friends.

  • Mari. She is Omori's older sister, and she loves him very much. She is very kind and playful and she has a crush on Hero. She is also a perfectionist and has very high standards for herself. She plays the piano and has a bad knee because of an accident when she was playing softball.

Real world

  • After the accident (see story), Sunny becomes an outcast. He stops going to school and eventually outside at all, and he spends all of his time sleeping and daydreaming about a world in which his friends are eral. He also does not speak and is shown to be quiet and fearful. He does not seem to take good care of himself. He is now 16.

  • Hero was really close to Mari, so her death hit him hard. He started studying to distract himself. He is still a high achiever and now goes to college. He has a good relationship with his brother but doesn't talk to the other kids. He is now 19.

  • Aubrey becomes angry when the friend group drifts apart after Mari's death. She starts bullying Basil and becomes the leader of a gang. However, she still misses her friends and doesn't truly hate them. She's 16.

  • Kel appears to be his usual easygoing self. He is very kind to Sunny and is a bit naive and immature. After the accident, he focused on sports and made friends there. He still looks up to his brother. He is 16.

  • After the accident, Basil is overcome with fear and guilt at what he has done. He becomes withdrawn and loses all of his friends (partly due to Aubrey.) He is shown to be shy and scared, and doesn't enjoy talking to people. He also stops most of his old hobbies, including taking care of his plants. He misses Sunny and relies on him, because Sunny is the only other person that knows the truth.
