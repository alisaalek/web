Who are the characters?

The main character, as stated above, is Iki Hiyori. The other two main characters are Yato and Yukine. Yato is the small god that promised to help Hiyori, and Yukine is his shinki. Other characters include Ebisu Kofuku, the goddess of poverty and a friend of Yato's, and her shinki Daikoku; Bishamon, a major god of war that has a grudge against Yato (who is actually a real god in Chinese Buddhism), and Kazuma, her shinki that is in dept of Yato; Tenjin, the god of knowledge and a minor character, and Nora, a shinki that has many masters and is considered disloyal and hated. Nora also has a past with Yato.
